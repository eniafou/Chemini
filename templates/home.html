About Chemini

...

Chemini is an advanced collaborative platform that leverages AI to facilitate real-time discussions for refining ideas. Users can customize metrics on-the-fly, ensuring efficient batch validation of ideas. The tool incorporates moonshot detection to emphasize groundbreaking concepts, and its user-friendly interface allows easy navigation and result sharing. Beyond idea validation, Chemini fosters a collaborative environment for ongoing improvement and innovation.
